## Flavor Text
<!--
  Short italicized lines that print on individual cards. No mechanical weight — pure voice and atmosphere.

  Format: one card name as a header, candidate line(s) beneath it in blockquote.
  Multiple candidates per card is fine — pick the best at print time.

  Guidelines:
  - Written in the character's voice, or about them from an outside perspective
  - One or two sentences max — if it needs a third, cut the first
  - Tone should match the Legend: deadpan, punchy, absurd, never tragic
  - Each line should feel distinct — avoid repeating the same joke structure across cards
  - Fabled Gift flavor text can be left TBD until gifts are named and finalized

  Name/flavor relationship:
  - The card name sets up the angle. The flavor text pays it off. They should be in dialogue.
  - Don't restate the name — if the card is called "Pitchfork Collection," don't write about collecting pitchforks. Write about what that implies.
  - Gut check: if the flavor text would work on any other card in the deck, it's not specific enough.
