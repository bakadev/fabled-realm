## Jekyll

**Archetype:** Mage / Wizard

**Playstyle:** Calculated, methodical, and Mana hungry. Builds resources patiently, sets up powerful combos, controls the board through careful spell play. High Mana generation, powerful Mana-gated Fabled Gifts. Quietly terrifying — opponents always know something big is coming.

**Deck profile:** High Mana generation, powerful Mana-gated Fabled Gifts, moderate card economy.

---

## Hyde

**Archetype:** Rogue

**Playstyle:** Explosive, unpredictable, and opportunistic. Chains attacks rapidly, targets weakened opponents, burns through cards fast. Comes out of nowhere and dismantles someone in a single turn.

**Deck profile:** High Attack output, Draw-heavy to sustain aggression, low survivability.

---

## The Transformation Card

The centerpiece of the deck — a single card that flips the character's identity mid-game.

**Concepts under consideration:**
- Jekyll can only transform when he has accumulated enough Mana, making transformation a deliberate tactical choice
- Hyde transforms back to Jekyll involuntarily if his Vitality drops too low — the beast losing control
- Each form has access to different Fabled Gifts, giving the player two skill sets to master
- The Transformation card is unplayable by other characters — a truly unique mechanic

*This character is still in active development. Fabled Gifts and card specifics TBD.*
