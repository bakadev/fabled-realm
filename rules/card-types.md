# Card Symbols

**Attack** — Deal damage equal to the number of Attack symbols to any target player or their Guard card. You may freely choose any opponent to attack.

**Guard** — Play this card in front of you. Each Guard symbol absorbs one point of incoming damage. Place one Wound Token on the card for each point of damage it absorbs. When Wound Tokens equal the number of Guard symbols, the Guard card is destroyed and moved to your Discard Pile. Any excess damage carries over to your Vitality or your next Guard card.

**Heal** — Recover one Vitality per symbol. Vitality cannot exceed your character's starting maximum.

**Draw** — Draw one card per symbol.

**Chain** — Play one additional card from your hand per symbol. Resolving Chain is mandatory — you must play a card for each symbol triggered this turn. If you run out of cards mid-chain, immediately draw two cards and continue.

**Mana** — Gain one Mana token per symbol. Mana tokens are kept in front of you and carry over between turns indefinitely. Spend Mana to activate special abilities or Mana-gated card effects.

---

## Mana

Mana is a deliberate resource, not an automatic one. You only gain Mana by playing cards that feature the Mana symbol, which means choosing to forgo other actions like attacking or defending that turn.

Mana tokens persist between turns with no maximum. Spend them to activate your character's more powerful abilities when the time is right.

Some cards have a basic effect and a separate Mana-gated effect. If you cannot or choose not to pay the Mana cost, the basic effect still resolves — only the Mana-gated portion is skipped.

---

## Fabled Gifts

Each character has unique abilities printed on certain cards that reflect their legendary nature. These Fabled Gifts set each character apart and give them their distinct playstyle. Some Fabled Gifts require Mana to activate.
