# Turn Structure

On your turn, do the following in order:

1. **Draw** one card from your Draw Pile. If your Draw Pile is empty, shuffle your Discard Pile into a new Draw Pile.
2. **Play** one card from your hand. Playing a card is mandatory.
3. **Resolve** all symbols on the card you played.
4. **Discard** the played card unless it is a Guard card, which stays in front of you until destroyed.
5. **End** your turn. Play passes to the left.

If you ever empty your hand completely, immediately draw two cards.
