# Glossary

## Card Icons

**Attack** — Offensive action cards used to deal damage to opponents.

**Guard** — Defensive cards that absorb incoming damage via Wound Tokens.

**Heal** — Cards that restore Vitality.

**Draw** — Pull a card from your Draw Pile.

**Chain** — Play cards sequentially, one after another in a single turn.

**Mana** — A per-player resource accumulated during play, used to play cards or activate abilities.

---

## Character & Game Terms

**Fabled Gift** — A unique ability belonging to a specific legend. Each character has their own set of Fabled Gifts that define their playstyle.

**Vitality** — A character's life total. When Vitality hits zero, that player is Fallen.

**Wound Tokens** — Damage markers placed on Guard cards when they absorb an attack.

**Fallen** — The state of a player whose Vitality has reached zero. A Fallen player is eliminated from the game.

**Draw Pile** — The face-down stack of cards a player draws from during their turn.

**Discard Pile** — The face-up stack where played or discarded cards are placed.

**Character Sheet** — A reference card for each legend listing their stats, Fabled Gifts, and special rules.
