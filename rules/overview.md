# Game Overview

## Summary

Fabled Realm: Friendly Fire is a 2+ player fantasy card game where iconic monsters and legendary figures, reimagined as D&D archetypes, battle for survival. Players take turns playing cards to attack, defend, and outmaneuver their opponents. The last player standing wins.

## Players
- Minimum: 2
- Maximum: No upper limit
- *Note: Special mechanics for larger groups (ganging up, alliances, etc.) are TBD and may be introduced at a certain player count threshold.*

## Vitality Scale

Inspired by Dungeon Mayhem's flat 10 HP model, Fabled Realm uses a slightly wider range to reflect each character's archetype:

| Type | Vitality |
|------|----------|
| Glass cannon | 8 |
| Standard | 10–12 |
| Tanky | 14 |

## Objective

Last player with Vitality remaining wins. If all remaining players are Fallen simultaneously, the game ends in a tie and a rematch is played.

## Setup

- Each player chooses a character and takes their corresponding deck, Vitality tracker, Vitality token, and Character Sheet.
- Each character starts with a different number of Vitality points based on their archetype, listed on their Character Sheet.
- Place your Vitality token on your starting Vitality value.
- Shuffle your deck. Each player draws three cards.
- The oldest player goes first.
